Pixora
Image Processing Workspace
An image processing workspace for analysis, transformation, segmentation and evaluation.
Palette
Color Conversion
Convert between RGB, Grayscale, Binary, CMY, CMYK and Indexed color spaces.
Histogram Analysis
Visualize intensity distribution, equalize, stretch and analyze contrast.
Filter
Filtering
Apply LPF, HPF and non-linear filters. Get MSE and PSNR quality metrics.
Cut
Segmentation
Threshold, edge detection, clustering (K-Means, FCM) and deep learning methods.
Analytics
Evaluation Metrics
Load a ground truth mask to compute F1, Precision, Recall, IoU and Accuracy.
Compress
Compression
Lossless PNG or lossy JPEG compression with ratio and size stats.
How to use
1 Click Load Image in the top right to upload your image.
2 Use the right panel to apply operations — each section is collapsible.
3 The processed result appears on the right side of the main view.
4 Open Metrics in the navbar to see quality analysis and segmentation evaluation.
